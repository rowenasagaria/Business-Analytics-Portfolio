Capstone Project Proposal: CompareWEB

Unified Marketplace for Used Goods

Overview

CompareWEB aims to revolutionize how people buy and sell used goods in Canada. Our platform aggregates listings from multiple platforms, providing a seamless experience for users to find the best deals on a wide variety of pre-owned items quickly and efficiently. This project will address the challenges faced by both buyers and sellers in the current fragmented used goods market.

Customer Segment

  • Customer Jobs:
    • Find affordable pre-owned goods (household items, electronics, furniture, cars).
    • Compare multiple listings easily without jumping through platforms.
    • Save time during the product search process.
    • Have confidence in buying decisions with a reliable, consolidated platform.
  • Pains:
    • Fragmented platforms require significant time to navigate and compare.
    • Lack of reliable filtering options across different websites.
    • Missed opportunities due to listings being spread across multiple platforms.
    • Difficulty in adapting to different platforms, especially for newcomers to Canada.
  • Gains:
    • All listings in one easy-to-use platform.
    • Advanced filtering options.
    • Personalized experience with alerts for specific items.
    • Time savings and convenience in finding reliable, affordable products.

Value Proposition

  • Products & Services:
    • Centralized website to integrate listings from multiple platforms.
    • Advanced filtering options based on category, price, location, and product condition.
    • Personalized alerts for new listings matching user preferences.
    • Web scraping and API integrations to keep listings updated.
  • Pain Relievers:
    • Aggregates product listings from platforms like Facebook Marketplace, Kijiji, Clutch, Autotrader.
    • Makes the product search more organized, reducing frustration and time spent.
    • Provides clear, comparable product options under one roof.
  • Gain Creators:
    • Offers ease of access, enabling quick browsing for pre-owned products.
    • Enhances user satisfaction by reducing the hassle of searching across different platforms.
    • Personalized alerts allow users to stay up-to-date with preferred listings.
    • A streamlined experience aimed at new immigrants, students, and migrants.
